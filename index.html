I have been a Lakers fan since the late 90s. They have won a total of 5 championships since I have started watching them and before that they had won 11.

This year is the first year back to the playoffs and many are excited. They played Trailblazers in the first round and won the series 4-1.

Many thought they would lose to them some even said they would get swept.

Here we see Charles Barkley from TNT admitting that he was wrong saying the Trailblazers would sweep the Lakers.

Its an exciting time for boxing right now especially in the lightweight division. On October 17, 2020 Vasyl Lomachenko will face-off against

young and up and coming superstar Teofimo Lopez, for the IBF, WBA, WBO, and WBC championships to try and become the undisputed champion in that division.

They both have great styles that make this a must watch fight.
